This tour takes you to sounds that connect and create New York City.

City-wide sounds compared to local sounds are not unique to a specific place, although they can be dependant on the environemnt (like the presence of a metro station in the area). What makes those sounds special is that they link most on New York City's citiznes together even if they don't share similalr daily experiences or live in the same small area. Those sounds are what make NYC as a whole an accoustic community.
